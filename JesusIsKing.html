Danceability: How suitable the track is for dancing. A value of 0 is least danceable and 1 is most danceable.
Energy: How fast, loud, and noisy the track feels. A value of 0 is not energetic and 1 is extremely energetic.
Valence: How positive the track is musically. A value of 0 is not positive at all and 1 is extremely positive.
Tempo: Estimated speed or pace of the track in Beats Per Minute (BPM).
Duration: Length of the track in minutes and seconds.
